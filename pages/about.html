Our Story

Banjul Electronics was founded in 2019 with a simple mission: to provide Gambians with access to quality, genuine electronics at fair prices.

What started as a small phone shop has grown into one of the most trusted electronics stores in the country. today, we serve hundreds of customers every week, from students buyding their first laptop to businesses setting up their offices.
